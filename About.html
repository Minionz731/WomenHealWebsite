About

Founded in 2022 the Women Heal Foundation began as a grassroots initiative to address the growing need for mental health support among women. What started as a small support group has evolved into a leading organization dedicated to empowering women through comprehensive mental health services and educational programs. Over the years, we’ve expanded our reach, launched community-driven projects, and hosted numerous workshops, touching the lives of countless women. Key milestones include the launch of our online counselling platform in 2025 and the opening of our first women’s wellness center in the same year too

The Women Heal Foundation is dedicated to empowering women by providing essential mental health support, educational resources, and community-driven programs. Our mission is rooted in the belief that "You heal a woman, you heal a nation." We strive to address the mental, emotional, and social challenges women face by offering counselling services, educational workshops, and support groups. Through our initiatives, we aim to foster resilience, promote self-empowerment, and create a supportive network that helps women thrive in every aspect of their lives. Our services are designed to uplift women, enabling them to overcome obstacles, achieve their goals, and contribute positively to their communities.

Carole N. Moloi is the visionary behind the Women Heal Foundation, dedicated to empowering women through mental health advocacy and support. With a background in Computer Science and media studies, Carole has been a driving force in creating a safe space for women to heal and thrive.

Carole's personal journey through tough and unspeakable challenges inspired her to build this platform. Having navigated her own struggles without the support she wished she had, Carole saw the need to create a community where women can come together, share their experiences, and heal with the help of one another. This foundation is her way of offering the support and understanding she longed for during her most difficult times.
