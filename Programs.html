Mental Health Counselling

Description of Services: Professional mental health counselling services designed to support women through various life challenges.

Eligibility: Available to all women aged 18 and older.

How to Access: Schedule an appointment through our website intake form.

Educational Workshops

Topics covered: Mental health awareness, stress management, self-care practices, financial literacy, career development, and more.

Schedule: Monthly workshops, both one-time events and series.

Registration Information: Register online through our website.

Support Groups

Description of Support Groups: Safe spaces for women to share experiences and connect with others facing similar challenges.

How to Join: Sign up through our website for weekly meetings.

Body Positivity & Self-Esteem Building

Description of Services: Workshops, support groups, and coaching designed to challenge negative body image and promote self-love.

Eligibility: Open to all women struggling with body image issues or low self-esteem.

How to Access: Sign up through our website for a mix of group workshops and individual coaching sessions.

Advocacy

Information on Advocacy Efforts: Public awareness campaigns, policy advocacy, and community outreach.

How to Get Involved: Join our advocacy network, volunteer for campaign activities, or donate to support our work.
